About

RPG Random Encounters is a web application that generates random encounters for tabletop role-playing games. It's intended to help game masters enrich their games by presenting dynamic content and encouraging emergent storytelling.

You can create games, add and edit custom encounters in addition to the preset content. The app is free to use.
